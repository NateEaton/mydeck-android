# Highlights

The Highlights screen is a global index of every highlight you've saved across all your bookmarks. It's the perfect place to review your research, find a specific quote, or jump back into a deep dive.

## Creating Highlights

Highlights are created in the reading view. Select text in an article or transcript and tap **Highlight** in the Android text selection menu, then choose a color and optionally enter a note. See the [Reading](./reading.md) guide for the full details on creating, editing, and deleting highlights.

## Opening Highlights

Tap the **menu icon** (☰) in the top-left corner to open the navigation drawer and tap **Highlights**.

Highlights are fetched from your Readeck server. The list opens from saved local highlights immediately and refreshes in the background while you browse.

## Browsing Highlights

Highlights stay grouped by bookmark. Groups and highlights are ordered by the newest highlight by default.

Each highlight card shows:
- The date and time the highlight was created
- The highlighted text (italicized)
- Any note you've added to the highlight

Tapping a highlight card navigates directly to that bookmark's reading view and scrolls automatically to the specific highlight. Tapping the bookmark title below a group of highlights opens that bookmark at your last reading position.

To edit or delete a highlight, tap its card to open the bookmark reader, then tap the highlighted text.

## Searching and Filtering

Tap the **filter icon** (≡) in the top bar to open the search and filter controls.

- **Search** — Type to search. By default, MyDeck searches highlight text, notes, bookmark titles, and site names.
- **Search Targets** — Tap the chips (Text, Notes, Title, Site) to narrow your search to specific fields.
- **Color Filter** — Filter highlights by their saved color. Choose **Any color** (default), **Yellow**, **Red**, **Blue**, **Green**, or **None** (highlights saved without a color).
- **Note Filter** — Filter by whether a highlight has a note (Any, With notes, or Without notes).

Tap **Clear filters** to remove all active search and filter criteria.

## Sorting

Tap the **sort icon** (↕) in the top bar to toggle between newest-first and oldest-first order. This reorders both the groups (by their most recent highlight) and the highlights within each group.

## Sync and Refresh

MyDeck keeps your highlights in sync with your Readeck server.
- Pull down on the list to refresh highlights immediately.
- A **progress bar** at the top shows when a refresh is in progress.
- If a refresh fails, saved highlights remain visible and a **Retry** button appears.
- If a background sync fails partway through, a banner indicates highlights may be incomplete — pull to refresh to retry.
- When you reopen downloaded content while online, MyDeck checks whether the server-side highlights have changed and refreshes the content if needed.
