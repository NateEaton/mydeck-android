Initial MyDeck release. Major changes from the upstream ReadeckApp fork include:
- OAuth Device Code Grant authentication
- Pocket-style navigation (My List, Archive, Favorites)
- Multiple list layouts (Grid, Compact, Mosaic)
- Full label management and filtering
- Dual reading modes (Article and Original webview)
- Reading progress tracking with auto-mark-as-read
- Typography customization panel
- Find in Article search
- Revised sync model with configurable content sync modes
- Tablet and landscape adaptive layouts
- Material Design 3 throughout
